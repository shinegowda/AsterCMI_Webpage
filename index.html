Bone Marrow Transplant(BMT) better known as haematopoietic stem cell transplant(HSCT) as bone marrow cells are transplanted

Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a healthy donor

Medicines and in certain situation radiotherapy as well is given to get rid of diseased marrow and bone marrow cells from healthy is given to replace it

IN autologous bone marrow transplant,high dose chemotherapy is given to kill cancer cells and child own marrow cells are given back

What are the types of Bone Marrow Transplant?

Autologous Bone Marrow Transplant- Marrow cells are collected from the patient's own body and transfused back after high dose chemotherapy

Autologous Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Sibling Donor)- Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically(HLA) matched sibling donor

Autologous Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Unrelated Donor)- Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically(HLA) matched donor obtained from bone marrow registries in India and abroad

Haplo-Identical Bone Marrow Tranplant - Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from the parent

Umbilical cord Bone Marrow Transplant - Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched umbilical cord donor obtained from umbilical cord registries in India and abroad

The warning signs of Immune deficiency

Failure to thrive - not gaining weight and height as per the age norns

Family history of death of children at young age due to immune deficiency

Repeated skin infections

Infections warranting multiple hospitalizations

Repeated episodes of diarrhea

Repeated abscess information (liver abscess,brain abscess)

Requirement of intravenous antibiotics to clear infections

2 or more episodes of sinus infections within a year

2 or more episodes of pneumonia

2 or more episodes of ear discharge

What are the conditions that requireBone Marrow Tranplant?

BMT is the treatment of choice for blood disorders like Thalassemia,Sikle Cell anemia,bone marrow failure syndromes like Aplastic anemia,serious immune deficiencies and certain genetic metabolic disorders.

It is also used to treat high risk cancers like some cases of Acute Lymphobiastic Leaukemia,Acute Myeloid Leukemia,Multiple Meoloma and solid tumours like Neuroblastoma,Ewings Sarcoma and certain relapsed cancers like Lymphomas
